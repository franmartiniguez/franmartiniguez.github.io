Part 1.2.3

The denoised images look like a blob of white at the center. The reason for this is because the MSE tries to minimize the distance between the prediction and the actual image. Since the model is prediciting just basically a random image, the best thing to do in order to minimize the loss is just predict a blob of white at the center, since that's what's closest to any random digit.
